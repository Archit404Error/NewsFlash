What is this site?
NewsFlash is designed so you can learn everything you need to know about an issue in just a few minutes. It automatically pulls and summarizes news articles from various sources, allowing you to read them over and understand the topic you want to learn more about. The site also identifies the biases of each source, letting you see opinions on both sides of the aisle.
